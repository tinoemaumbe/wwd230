Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Tinoe Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

The site name for this chamber of commerce project is Tinoe Chamber of Commerce. The name represents the local community in a ceratin city in which the chamber operates and promotes local businesses.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the website is to serve as a regional economic catalyst, leadership convener, and community champion. The site will provide member services, promote economic and community development, and support public policy that balances economic prosperity.

Two main purposes for developing and running this website are:

  1. To provide a platform for members to access resources, network with other businesses, and promote their services as well as their products also.
  2. To offer information about the local community, including events, demographics, and economic opportunities.

Scenarios

Scenarios represent questions and needs that the target audience of the chamber of commerce site might have. These scenarios will shape the content and information provided on the website. Here are three example scenarios:

  1. What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  2. Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  3. What has been the population growth in the area?

Audience

This website is targetting everyone in a community whose business is at stake and want to increase his/her profits as well as those that provide services to the community.
